Kolmogorov’s inequality


Let X1,…,Xn be independent random variables in a probability space, such that E⁡[Xk]=0 and Var⁡[Xk]<∞ for k=1,…,n. Then, for each λ>0,

P(max1≤k≤n|Sk|≥λ)≤1λ2Var[Sn]=1λ2∑k=1nVar[Xk],

where Sk=X1+⋯+Xk.
